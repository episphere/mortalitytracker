Taming CDC's WONDER - The Center for Disease Control (CDC) maintains a set of WONDER online databases for the analysis of public health data, including causes of death. The main purpose of this library is to 1) provide a parser for WONDER text reports and 2) facilitate the programatic operations of WONDER's API.
